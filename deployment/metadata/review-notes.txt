APP REVIEW INFORMATION

TESTING THE APP:

No account or login required. The app works entirely offline after initial exchange rate download.

REQUIRED PERMISSIONS:
- Camera Access: Required for scanning price tags. The app will request permission on first use of the scanner.

BASIC WORKFLOW TO TEST:
1. Grant Camera access when prompted
2. Point camera at any price tag or number
3. The app will automatically recognize and convert the price
4. Tap on highlighted text to select specific prices
5. Use the Calculator tab for manual entry
6. Tap "Calculator" button to send scanned amount to calculator

TESTING SCANNER:
- Point camera at any printed price (newspaper, receipt, menu, etc.)
- Gold brackets show the scan region
- Recognized text is highlighted
- Conversion appears automatically in the header
- Tap on specific highlighted price to select it

TESTING CALCULATOR:
- Enter any amount using the keypad
- Conversion updates in real-time
- Use % button for percentage calculations
- Tap currency flags to change source/destination currencies

TESTING CURRENCY SELECTION:
- Tap either currency flag in the header
- Search or scroll to find currencies
- 150+ currencies supported
- Tap swap button to reverse conversion direction

TESTING EXCHANGE RATES:
- Go to Settings tab
- Tap "Update Rates Now" to fetch latest rates
- Rates are cached for offline use
- Last updated timestamp shown

PRIVACY & DATA:
- Camera feed is processed on-device only
- No images are stored or uploaded
- No accounts or personal data collected
- No analytics or tracking
- Exchange rates fetched from open.er-api.com (free public API)

KNOWN LIMITATIONS:
- Requires iOS 16 or later (VisionKit DataScanner requirement)
- Requires device with Neural Engine (iPhone XS or later)
- Camera scanning works best with clear, printed text

SUBSCRIPTION INFORMATION:
- Pro subscription ($2.99/month) unlocks camera scanning
- Free users have access to the calculator with currency conversion
- 3-day free trial included for new subscribers
- To test: tap "Start Free Trial" on the locked scanner screen

Thank you for reviewing Real Time Currency Conversion!
