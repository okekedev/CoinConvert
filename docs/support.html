Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert currencies?

Use the Calculator tab to enter an amount. Select your source currency (the currency you have) and destination currency (the currency you want to convert to) by tapping on the flags. The conversion happens automatically in real-time.

How do I use the price scanner?

The price scanner is a Pro feature. Once subscribed:
1. Go to the Scan tab
2. Point your camera at any price tag
3. The app will automatically detect the price and show the conversion
4. Tap "Calculator" to use the scanned amount in the calculator

How do I change currencies?

Tap on either flag in the conversion header to open the currency picker. You can search for currencies by name or code. Tap the swap button (arrows) between the flags to quickly swap source and destination currencies.

How accurate are the exchange rates?

Exchange rates are fetched from reliable financial data providers and updated regularly. However, actual rates at banks or exchange services may vary slightly. The app is intended for estimation purposes.

What's included in the Pro subscription?

  • Camera price scanning - point at any price tag for instant conversion
  • Automatic price detection in any currency
  • Real-time conversion overlay while scanning
  • 7-day free trial, then $1.99/month

How do I start my free trial?

Go to the Scan tab and tap "Start Free Trial". You'll have 7 days of full Pro access. You won't be charged until the trial ends, and you can cancel anytime.

How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Open Settings on your iPhone
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Select CoinConvert Pro
5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

How do I restore my purchase?

If you've previously subscribed on this Apple ID, tap "Restore Purchases" on the Scan tab to restore your Pro access.

The camera scanner isn't focusing properly

Try these tips:
- Ensure adequate lighting on the price tag
- Hold your phone steady about 6-12 inches from the price
- Tap on the screen to manually focus on the price area
- Make sure the price text is within the scanning brackets
