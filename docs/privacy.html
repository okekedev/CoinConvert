Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 28, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to CoinConvert ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

CoinConvert is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you. Specifically:

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No Usage Analytics: We do not track how you use the app or collect analytics data.
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect any information, we don't use, share, or sell any of your data.

Camera Access

The Pro version of CoinConvert uses your device's camera for the price scanning feature. Important details:

  • Camera access is used solely for real-time text recognition to detect prices
  • No images or video are stored, recorded, or transmitted
  • All processing happens locally on your device
  • Camera data never leaves your device

Exchange Rate Data

The app fetches current exchange rates from a third-party API to provide accurate currency conversions. This request does not include any personal information or device identifiers.

In-App Purchases

Our app offers a Pro subscription through Apple's In-App Purchase system. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple may collect data related to your purchases according to their own privacy policy.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera Access (Pro feature): Required for the price scanning feature. This permission is used solely for real-time text recognition and no images are stored.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
